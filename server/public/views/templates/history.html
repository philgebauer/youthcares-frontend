HISTORY

Camp Sunrise

YouthLEAD Program

Young Womens Mentoring Program

  • 1974
    YouthCARE was started as the Wilderness Work Camp, a summer work and camp program for youth working in the Twin Cities summer youth employment programs.
  • 1977
    In 1977 the name was changed to Camp Sunrise and we initiated the YouthLEAD program to meet the school year needs of the youth involved in the summer program.
  • 1990
    In 1990, the Youth Womens Mentoring Program was established to provide opportunities for you women to gain meaningful employment opportunities and develop their leadership skills as they mentored younger girls.
  • 1993
    In 1993, the oranizations name was changed from Camp Sunrise to YouthCARE (Youth for Cultural Appreciation and Racial Equality) in order to more accurately reflect the organizations commitment to cultral appreciation and racial equality.
